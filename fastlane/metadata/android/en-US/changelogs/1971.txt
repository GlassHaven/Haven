- Fix arrow keys not working in Mutt (and other ncurses-based apps)
  over Mosh (#73). The mosh client was sending each byte of an
  escape sequence like `ESC O A` in its own protobuf instruction,
  and mosh-server was writing them to the pty in separate calls.
  ncurses' ESC-timeout interpreted the gaps as a bare ESC followed
  by O and A, and Mutt showed "key is not bound". Consecutive
  keystroke bytes are now coalesced into a single instruction,
  matching upstream mosh-client's `UserStream::diff_from`.
