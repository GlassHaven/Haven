v5.1.3: VNC VeNCrypt + HLS video streaming fix

VNC:
- Add VeNCrypt (RFB security type 19) with TLSPlain, X509Plain,
  TLSVnc, X509Vnc, TLSNone, X509None, and Plain sub-types (#76)
- Connects to wayvnc, TigerVNC, libvncserver, x11vnc servers that
  require a username in addition to a password
- Wraps the socket in TLS after sub-type selection — all RFB traffic
  after authentication is encrypted
- Add vncUsername field to connection profiles (DB migration 31→32)

HLS media streaming:
- Fix video streaming failing with MEDIA_ERR_SRC_NOT_SUPPORTED on
  Chrome: force H.264 Main profile level 4.0, cap at 1920x1080,
  cap at 30fps, yuv420p pixel format. Portrait screen recordings
  (e.g. 1344x2992 @ 55fps) now play correctly.
- HLS player starts muted to satisfy Chrome's autoplay policy,
  with a "Tap to unmute" overlay button
- Playlist is now properly marked as VOD with #EXT-X-PLAYLIST-TYPE
- Removed append_list hls_flag (was producing spurious
  EXT-X-DISCONTINUITY markers)
- Silent -an flag for video inputs with no audio track (avoids the
  "b:a unused" warning)
