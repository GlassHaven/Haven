v5.4.1: Security hardening

- SFTP loopback bridge requires a random per-session
  token, blocking other apps from reading SFTP files
  via the local port. CORS wildcard removed.
- Biometric app-lock no longer auto-unlocks when the
  device biometric is unenrolled.
- FileProvider <root-path> dropped; the credential DB
  is no longer reachable via FileProvider URIs.
- SSH passphrases held as CharArray and wiped on
  teardown; passwords no longer pass through String.
