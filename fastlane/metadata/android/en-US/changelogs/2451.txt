v5.12.0: image tools — view, crop, rotate, perspective

Long-press any image file in the SFTP browser → "Open in
image tools" to view, crop, rotate (90° steps), and fix
perspective (four-corner drag). Apply, preview, save back
through the active transport — SFTP, SCP, SMB, rclone, or
local.

Works the same way as the editor — no separate app, no
round-trip download.
