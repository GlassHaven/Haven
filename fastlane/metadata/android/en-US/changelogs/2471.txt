v5.14.0: chown in the SFTP file browser

Companion to the permissions editor shipped in v5.13. Open
the Owner dialog from a file's context menu or from the
selection top bar (person icon) — enter `user`, `group`, or
`user:group` and the remote runs `chown` directly.

Name resolution happens on the server, so either names or
numeric UIDs are accepted. Works over SFTP and SCP.
Typically requires root on the remote. SMB, rclone, and
local files are unaffected — those backends have no
ownership concept that maps cleanly.
