v5.16.0: drop Google Nearby Connections (F-Droid compatibility)

Removes a transitive `com.google.android.gms:play-services-nearby`
dependency from the `reticulum-kt` submodule. That dep was pulled
into every Haven APK via `rns-android` even though Haven never
instantiated the Nearby driver — so this is pure dead-weight
removal:

- Slightly smaller APK (one fewer Google library linked).
- Fewer runtime permissions requested (BLUETOOTH_* remain for BLE;
  ACCESS_WIFI_STATE / CHANGE_WIFI_STATE / NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES gone).
- Unblocks F-Droid publishing — the F-Droid scanner rejects any
  `com.google.android.gms` artifact.

The abstract Nearby transport interfaces in `rns-interfaces` stay.
No Haven-visible feature changes.
