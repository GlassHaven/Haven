v5.18.0: secure keyboard — Voice + Raw toolbar keys (#70, #96)

Two new toolbar keys for controlling how much of the IME surface the
terminal exposes. Both are off by default; add them to your toolbar
(long-press the keyboard icon → reorder → drag in).

• Voice — enables Gboard voice input, swipe, and autocomplete. Same
  state as Settings → Keyboard → Use standard keyboard, but reachable
  without leaving the terminal.
• Raw — returns no input connection to the IME at all. Gboard has
  nothing to attach to, so its microphone, suggestion strip, and AI
  Core writing assist cannot appear. Physical keyboards still work;
  soft-keyboard input comes through as raw key events only. No IME
  composition, so no CJK.

Raw and Voice are mutually exclusive; turning one on auto-clears the
other. Terminal mode (default) now also sets
IME_FLAG_NO_PERSONALIZED_LEARNING unconditionally, which tells
Gboard's word bank and AI Core to keep their hands off the terminal
field — not enough on its own against the most recent Gboard /
Android 16 patch combination (hence Raw).
