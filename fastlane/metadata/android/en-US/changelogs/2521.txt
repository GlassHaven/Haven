v5.19.0: fix Voice mode not re-enabling Gboard mic (#70)

Follow-up to v5.18.0. The Voice toolbar key was setting the
preference correctly but the microphone still didn't come back on
Android 16 + AI Core. Root cause: v5.18.0 set
IME_FLAG_NO_PERSONALIZED_LEARNING unconditionally, and recent
Gboard builds treat that flag as strong enough to also suppress the
microphone — even when the field is otherwise a full text input.

v5.19.0 only sets the flag when you're in secure / terminal mode.
Flipping Voice on now drops the flag, and Gboard shows mic +
suggestion strip as expected. Raw mode still fully hides Gboard
(it can't decorate a field that doesn't exist).
