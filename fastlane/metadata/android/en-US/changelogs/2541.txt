v5.20.0-rc2: clamp selection Copy/Paste row (#98)

Small UX fix on top of rc1's floating IME composer.

When you long-press text in the terminal, a small Copy/Paste
button row appears above the selection. The buttons were
anchored at `endColumn × charWidth`, with no right-edge clamp —
so a selection that ended near the right of the terminal pushed
the Row off-screen (@mio-19, #98). Now clamped within the
terminal's viewport on both axes.

No other behaviour changes from rc1.
