v5.20.1: wrapped URL smartcopy

Long-pressing a URL that's wrapped across two or more terminal
rows used to copy only the visible fragment on the row you
tapped — so a tap on "https://github.com/.../issues/89" that
wrapped at "...issues/89" gave you "https://github.com/..."
without the trailing "/issues/89".

The long-press word-expansion now walks across wrap continuation
rows when the joined text contains a URL scheme (://) or a
bare www. prefix, so the whole URL lands on the clipboard in
one shot. Non-URL words at row edges still get the single-row
word walk — adjacent prose isn't sprawled into.

Also landed: unit tests around the smart-copy pipeline so
future regressions in this area get caught before ship.
