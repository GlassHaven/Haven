v5.21.0-rc1: fast right-flick navigates up in Files (#89)

Adds a velocity-based right-flick gesture to the file browser:

- SLOW horizontal drag → passes through to the tab pager as
  before, swiping to the Terminal tab.
- FAST rightward flick in a subdirectory → navigates up one
  directory (same effect as tapping the ".." row).
- FAST flick at root → falls through to the pager (swipes to
  Terminal).

Uses Compose's Initial-pass pointer events so we observe the
drag without consuming it; a VelocityTracker only claims the
gesture once the finger has clearly flicked (≥200 dp/s, ≥40 dp
travel). Below that threshold the pager handles the drag
normally. Disabled during multi-select so a sideways flick
doesn't drop your selection.

RC release because the velocity feel may want further tuning
once more people have it in-hand. Please try it and feed back
on #89.
