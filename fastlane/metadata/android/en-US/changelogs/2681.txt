v5.24.4: smarter URL tap-to-open + scrollback-aware selection

Tap resolution for URLs treats the terminal as a 2D grid now. URLs
wrapped under Claude Code's `⎿` prefix, inside box-drawn panels,
across markdown table rows, or any scenario with formatting scaffolding
on continuation lines now resolve to the full URL — not just the
fragment on the tapped row.

Also fixes a scrollback bug that affected both URL taps and text
selection: when scrolled back, visible rows that mapped to the
current screen were being resolved against the scrollback buffer
instead, producing blank selections or wrong-row URL lookups.

13 new spec tests cover the URL detector (markdown tables, box
frames, formatting prefixes, convexity guards, false-positive
rejection); full termlib suite is 316/316 green.
