Haven v1.0.0

- SSH and Reticulum terminal with multi-tab support
- OSC support: clipboard (52), hyperlinks (8), notifications (9/777), CWD tracking (7)
- Open URL button in selection toolbar
- Terminal notifications (toast foreground, system notification background)
- Hack font for Unicode glyph support
- Mouse mode with SGR wheel events
- Session manager integration (tmux, screen, Zellij)
- Word-expand selection on long-press
