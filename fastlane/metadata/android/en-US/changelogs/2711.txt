v5.24.7: paste from selection popup actually reaches the remote

The small Paste button next to Copy and More in the text-selection popup
was writing clipboard text into the local terminal screen buffer instead
of sending it to the PTY. Pasted text rendered on screen but vim, nano,
tmux — anything running remotely — never saw it. If you saved and quit
nano after that kind of paste, your work went unwritten.

The Ctrl+Shift+V hardware shortcut and the keyboard-toolbar paste button
were always correct; the selection-popup button was the only broken
path, regressed since v5.15.0 (2026-03-31).

Bracketed-paste wrapping (mode 2004) now applies uniformly across all
three paste triggers.

Fixes #103 on GitHub.
