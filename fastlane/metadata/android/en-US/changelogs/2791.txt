v5.24.15: backup now preserves SSH-key passphrase flag

Confirmed during an audit that every ConnectionProfile field and every
tunnel_configs row round-trips through backup export/import correctly
— including the saved VNC settings (port, username, password,
tunnel-through-SSH) added in v5.24.10 and edited in v5.24.14.

One small residual gap: SshKey.isEncrypted — the flag marking whether
the private key material itself is passphrase-protected — wasn't in
the export/import schema. It defaulted to false on restore, which
could have prevented JSch from prompting for the passphrase on the
first use of a restored key. Now serialised and imported alongside
the other SshKey fields.

v1 and v2 backups still restore cleanly; the flag falls back to false
when the key is absent from the JSON, preserving the pre-fix
behaviour for older files.

Follow-up to #104: same audit verified that vncPort, vncUsername,
vncPassword, and vncSshForward have been in the backup since v5.24.10
— no action needed there.
