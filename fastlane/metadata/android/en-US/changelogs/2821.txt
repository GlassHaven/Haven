v5.24.18: smart-copy URLs with wrapped #fragments and ?query strings

The 2D URL blob detector from v5.24.4 would miss URLs whose continuation
row only carried the fragment or query part — e.g. a GitHub
issue-comment link wrapping as

    ⎿ https://github.com/org/repo/issues/
      106#issuecomment-4298664962

Long-pressing copied just the first row. Fix: accept `/`, `#`, `?`, `&`,
or `=` on the continuation row as structural evidence the URL carries
over, rather than requiring `/` specifically. These are the glyphs
English prose essentially never contains inside a URL-safe run.

Two new spec tests cover the behaviour — fragment-anchor wrap and
query-string wrap.
