v5.24.28: RDP touchpad parity (#107)

The Settings → "Touchpad input" toggle now applies to RDP sessions
too, not just VNC.

- DesktopTab.Rdp gains _pointerPos to drive the touchpad-mode
  virtual cursor (RDP doesn't transmit cursor-shape pseudo-encodings
  like VNC does, so the on-screen cursor is the system mouse pointer
  the server already paints into the framebuffer — no separate
  cursor decoder needed).
- DesktopViewModel.sendPointer / sendClick now track _pointerPos for
  RDP tabs.
- RdpScreen single-finger gesture handler forks on inputMode the
  same way VNC does: TOUCHPAD integrates screen-space deltas into
  virtualCursor (divided by zoom), tap clicks at the cursor instead
  of the finger, no button-1 press during cursor moves.
- Camera-follow ("Mario camera") wired via the same LaunchedEffect
  pattern; helper is duplicated in RdpScreen (feature/rdp doesn't
  depend on feature/vnc, and it's a small pure function).

v1 limitations carry over from VNC: drag-with-button isn't
supported in touchpad mode for either protocol — separate release
once the gesture convention is decided.
