v5.24.34: separate VNC/RDP gestures for remote scroll vs viewport pan

Two-finger drag was doing both at once: it scrolled the remote
*and* dragged the viewport. Trying to scroll a remote browser also
panned the framebuffer, and trying to pan the framebuffer also
fired a scroll wheel into whatever app was focused on the remote.
Reported on #107.

New gesture model — no toggle:
  - 1 finger: tap / drag / long-press as before
  - 2 fingers: remote mouse-wheel scroll only
  - 3 fingers: local viewport pan + pinch-zoom

Locked by max-fingers-seen during a gesture, so once you've used
three fingers (or two), dropping back doesn't re-purpose the
gesture mid-stroke. Same gesture model in both VNC and RDP
sessions.
