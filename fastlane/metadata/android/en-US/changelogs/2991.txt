v5.24.35: stop viewport jumping when 3 fingers lift (#107)

Follow-up to v5.24.34's three-finger gesture model. Lifting one
of the three fingers caused the viewport to jump: the centroid
(average position) was recomputed over the remaining two fingers,
so it teleported. The next frame fed that teleport in as a real
pan delta and the framebuffer skipped sideways.

Fix: track the pointer count between frames and skip delta
application on the frame where it changes. Pinch span gets the
same treatment so zoom doesn't snap on a finger lift either.
Same fix in VNC and RDP.
