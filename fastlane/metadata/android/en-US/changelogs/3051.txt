v5.24.41: hotfix — revert RDP colour-depth default to 16bpp (xrdp compat)

v5.24.40's "honour the requested colour depth" change had the
predicted xrdp regression: blank screen on local xrdp because
ironrdp doesn't decode xrdp's custom 32bpp RLE variant. Reverting
the Kotlin default from 32 → 16 restores the v5.24.39 behaviour.

Surf5726's request for smoother RDP updates (#109) needs the
per-profile colour-depth picker (mirror of the VNC one); that's
v5.24.42. Until then, RDP runs at 16bpp + interleaved RLE for all
servers — works everywhere, line-by-line repaints on Windows.
