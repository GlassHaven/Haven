v5.24.49: structured RDP error hint cards (#109)

Three new RdpErrorHint patterns turn the diagnostic strings shipped
in v5.24.47/v5.24.48 into top-level hint cards above the raw error
in the failed-connection screen:

- "Server rejected the CredSSP public-key hash" — for the
  gnome-remote-desktop / FreeRDP-server NLA interop case. Spells out
  the workaround inline (uncheck NLA on the profile) and links to the
  tracking issue.
- "Device clock differs from server's" — for CredSSP TimeSkew
  rejections, with the Settings → Date & time pointer.
- "Server requires TLS ciphers Haven doesn't support" — for the
  rustls/ring vs SChannel/OpenSSL cipher coverage gap that surfaces
  on hosts with narrow cipher configs.
