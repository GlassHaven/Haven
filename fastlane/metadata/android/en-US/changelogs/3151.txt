v5.24.51: hotfix — pin termlib submodule to MouseDrag commit (#94)

v5.24.50 added the drag-to-select-in-tmux feature on the Haven side
but the termlib submodule pointer wasn't updated to the commit that
defines TerminalGestureCallback.onMouseDrag and MouseDragPhase. CI
failed to compile feature/terminal because the symbols didn't exist
at the pinned termlib SHA. Local builds worked because the working
tree had the changes, masking the gap.

This release pins the submodule to termlib commit 8fca17c (which adds
the MouseDrag callback + gesture state) so the v5.24.50 feature
actually compiles and ships. Same code, just the right submodule pin.
