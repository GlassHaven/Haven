v5.24.61: shorter "Rename" label in the tab menu

The tab long-press "Rename session" item from v5.24.60 was wordier
than it needed to be — the menu's already attached to a specific tab
so "session" is implied. Switched the DropdownMenuItem text to the
existing common "Rename" string (already translated across all 11
locales as `common_rename`).

The icon-button rename in the picker dialog still uses the longer
`terminal_rename_session` for its content description — the picker
lists multiple sessions where "session" is the disambiguator, and
content descriptions are read aloud by screen readers and benefit
from the explicit context.
