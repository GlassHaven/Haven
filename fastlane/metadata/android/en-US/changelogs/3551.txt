v5.24.91: Material You terminal colour scheme (#139)

New "Material You" entry in Settings → Terminal → Colour scheme.
When selected, the terminal foreground / background pull from
`MaterialTheme.colorScheme.surface` / `onSurface` instead of a fixed
palette, so the terminal chrome follows the system's dynamic theme
(wallpaper-driven, switches with light / dark mode). Default stays
HAVEN to preserve existing users' look.

Limited to fg / bg in this round — the 16 ANSI colours and the
256-colour palette stay at xterm defaults. Harmonising the ANSI
colours with the dynamic scheme is a follow-up since picking a
palette that matches every wallpaper-derived hue without losing
SGR-code legibility takes more design care than dropping the
default scheme over the chrome.
