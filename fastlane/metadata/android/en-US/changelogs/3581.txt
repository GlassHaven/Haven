v5.24.94: FIDO2 PIN — fall back to legacy getPinToken on CTAP 2.0 keys (#15)

olmari (#15): YubiKey 5 with verify-required SK key returned CTAP2
INVALID_COMMAND (0x01) right after PIN entry, both on USB and NFC.

Cause: v5.24.84 always sent `getPinUvAuthTokenUsingPinWithPermissions`
(subCommand 0x09), a CTAP 2.1 feature. CTAP 2.0 / 2.1-PRE firmware (the
YubiKey 5 line shipped before mid-2021) only implements the legacy
`getPinToken` (subCommand 0x05); calling 0x09 on those keys returns
INVALID_COMMAND.

Fix: parse `options.pinUvAuthToken` from the GetInfo response and pick
the matching subCommand:

  • pinUvAuthToken: true   → 0x09 (with permissions, scoped to GetAssertion + rpId)
  • absent or false        → 0x05 (legacy, implicitly bound to all permissions)

Both v1 and v2 PIN protocols work on either subCommand; the crypto
(ECDH → derive → AES-CBC pinHashEnc → HMAC clientDataHash) is identical.
