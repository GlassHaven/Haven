v5.24.97: AgentActivityScreen overlay readability

The robot chip on the Connections top bar opens the agent audit log
as an overlay over the pager. The screen's root Column had no
background colour, so the Connections list bled through and made the
overlay illegible.

Fix: paint the Column with `MaterialTheme.colorScheme.background`.
Self-contained — Settings still renders the same screen via its own
mounting path; the explicit background is harmless there.
