Workspace profiles — bundle terminal sessions, file-browser tabs,
remote desktops and the Wayland tab into a named workspace and reopen
them all in one tap. Long-press the launcher icon for shortcuts to
your saved workspaces. The `compose_workspace` MCP verb lets agents
launch the same bundles.

Plus: fix an IndexOutOfBoundsException that could fire when tapping
the back button rapidly in the SFTP browser. Concurrent directory
listings raced on the JSch channel; they now serialise, and the
channel resets cleanly if it does get corrupted.
