FIDO2 SSH key over NFC: the touch prompt now shows transport-aware
copy. NFC users see "Hold your security key — keep it against the
back of the device for two seconds while it signs. Moving it away
early aborts the exchange and you'll need to tap it again." USB
users still get the existing "press the button" text.

Reported by @t3rr0rz0n3 in #15: SoloKey-via-NFC users were pulling
the key away as soon as the on-key LED indicated "ready", which
interrupted the 1–2s CTAP2 GetAssertion exchange and surfaced as
TagLostException → JSch's opaque "Auth fail for methods 'publickey'"
disconnect. The CTAP exchange itself is unchanged — this is the UX
fix that explains what to do.
